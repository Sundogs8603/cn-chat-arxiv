    这篇讲义试图用类似于数学物理学的方法对博弈论进行数学处理。一个博弈实例被定义为底层系统状态的序列。这个观点将二人博弈、组合和零和博弈的经典数学模型以及投资和赌博模型统一起来。重点研究了n人博弈的效用、潜力和均衡概念，这使得协作博弈成为特殊情况。通过在希尔伯特空间中表示博弈理论系统，建立了与量子力学数学模型和一般交互系统的联系。这份讲义概述了这一理论的概要，详细信息可在其他教材中找到。

    These lecture notes attempt a mathematical treatment of game theory akin to mathematical physics. A game instance is defined as a sequence of states of an underlying system. This viewpoint unifies classical mathematical models for 2-person and, in particular, combinatorial and zero-sum games as well as models for investing and betting. n-person games are studied with emphasis on notions of utilities, potentials and equilibria, which allows to subsume cooperative games as special cases. The represenation of a game theoretic system in a Hilbert space furthermore establishes a link to the mathematical model of quantum mechancis and general interaction systems.  The notes sketch an outline of the theory. Details are available as a textbook elsewhere.

    [http://arxiv.org/abs/2010.04385](http://arxiv.org/abs/2010.04385)

    本文研究了未观测到异质性的内生和多值离散处理设置中，识别连续结果的处理效果的充分条件，并确定了多值处理设置下的局部处理效应。

    In this paper, we establish sufficient conditions for identifying treatment effects on continuous outcomes in endogenous and multi-valued discrete treatment settings with unobserved heterogeneity. We employ the monotonicity assumption for multi-valued discrete treatments and instruments, and our identification condition has a clear economic interpretation. In addition, we identify the local treatment effects in multi-valued treatment settings and derive closed-form expressions of the identified treatment effects. We provide examples to illustrate the usefulness of our result.
